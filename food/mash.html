Basic Mashed Potato

Description

If you love good, old fashioned mashed potatoes this is the perfect recipe. Simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Two pounds of baking potato, peeled and quartered
  • Two tablespoons of butter
  • One cup of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes; drain.

  2. In a small saucepan heat butter and milk over low heat until butter is melted.

    Using a potato masher or electric beater, slowly blend milk mixture into potatoes until smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
